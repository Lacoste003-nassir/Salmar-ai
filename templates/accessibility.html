Accessibility at Salmar AI

At Salmar AI, accessibility is a core part of our vision. We believe that technology should empower everyone, regardless of ability, background, or circumstance. This page outlines how Salmar AI is designed to be inclusive and accessible for all users.

🔹 Accessible Design Principles

Salmar AI follows WCAG 2.1 AA and international accessibility standards. Our key principles include:

👁️

Perceivable

Clear fonts, optimal color contrast, and text-to-speech options ensure that information is accessible to all users, including those with visual impairments.

👆

Operable

Full keyboard navigation, voice commands, and adaptive UI make Salmar AI usable for everyone, regardless of input method.

🧠

Understandable

Simple instructions, consistent layouts, and intuitive flows ensure that all users can navigate and understand our platform easily.

⚙️

Robust

Full compatibility with assistive technologies like screen readers, magnifiers, and alternative input devices ensures reliable access for all.

🔹 Features for Accessibility

🎙️ Text-to-Speech & Speech-to-Text

  • Salmar can read out responses in a natural, human-like voice
  • Users can speak commands instead of typing, making interaction seamless and hands-free
  • Multiple language support for global accessibility

👁️ Visual Accessibility

  • High-contrast purple-on-dark theme optimized for low-vision users
  • Adjustable font sizes with fluid typography for comfortable reading
  • Option for dyslexia-friendly fonts (OpenDyslexic support)
  • Clear visual hierarchy and spacing for easy navigation

🎧 Hearing Accessibility

  • All sounds (like Salmar Orb hums) include visual indicators
  • Closed captions for all audio outputs
  • Visual feedback for all system notifications and alerts

⌨️ Motor Accessibility

  • Full keyboard navigation support with logical tab order
  • One-click shortcuts for common tasks and actions
  • Adaptive interface for eye-tracking and voice-only navigation
  • Large, easy-to-click interactive elements with generous spacing

💡 Cognitive Support

  • Clear, step-by-step instructions in our comprehensive Help Center
  • Simplified mode for users who prefer short, direct answers
  • Color-coded visual cues for different contexts (processing, ready, error)
  • Consistent interface patterns to reduce cognitive load

🔹 Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing journey, not a finished product. We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation.

  • Regular audits of Salmar AI's accessibility features by independent experts
  • Active feedback loops with users who rely on assistive technologies
  • Partnering with global accessibility organizations and advocacy groups
  • Implementing cutting-edge accessibility technologies as they emerge
  • Training our team on accessibility best practices and inclusive design
  • Publishing annual accessibility reports to track our progress

🔹 How to Request Accessibility Support

We're Here to Help

If you encounter barriers while using Salmar AI, please don't hesitate to reach out. We will work directly with you to find solutions and improve our platform.
