Privacy Policy

Your trust is the foundation of Salmar. Here’s how we protect your data and respect your privacy.

Introduction

Salmar is designed to serve you, not exploit you. We collect only what’s necessary, and you’re always in control.

Information We Collect
  • Accounts: Name, email, and login credentials.
  • Usage Data: Interactions, preferences, and feature activity.
  • Device Data: Browser, operating system, and IP (for security and fraud prevention).
How We Use Your Information
  • To provide and continuously improve Salmar’s features.
  • To personalize your experiences and recommendations.
  • To maintain security, detect anomalies, and prevent abuse.
Biometric & Security Data

Where enabled, you may use biometric login (e.g., fingerprint or Face ID) for convenient and secure access.

Assurance: Biometric data is stored securely on your device and never leaves it.

Data Sharing & Integrations

With your permission, Salmar can connect to services like Google, Slack, and Discord to deliver seamless workflows.

  • Integrations are scoped to the minimum necessary permissions.
  • Data is shared only to complete a requested action (e.g., posting a message on your behalf).

We do not sell your data. Ever.

Your Rights & Controls
  • Access, edit, or delete your account and data at any time.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Opt out of non-essential analytics and tracking.
Data Storage & Protection
  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data in transit and at rest.
  • Secure servers, strict access controls, and zero-trust principles.
  • Committed to global standards and compliance (GDPR, CCPA).
