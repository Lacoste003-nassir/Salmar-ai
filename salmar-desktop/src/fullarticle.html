Getting Started

Sign Up

Create your Salmar AI account in just a few simple steps.

  • Go to the Sign Up Page and enter your name, email, and password
  • Optionally enable biometric sign-up (face/fingerprint recognition)
  • Accept the Terms of Service & Privacy Policy
  • Confirm your email address via the verification link
  • Complete your profile and start exploring Salmar AI

Log In

Access your Salmar AI account securely with multiple authentication options.

  • Enter your email and password on the login page
  • Use biometric authentication (face or fingerprint) for quick access

  • Enable two-factor authentication for enhanced security

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a verification link to reset your password. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Is my data secure with Salmar AI?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all communications and data storage. Your conversations are private, and we never sell or misuse your data. You have complete control over your information and can delete it at any time.

Can I use Salmar AI on multiple devices?

Yes! Salmar AI works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Your conversations and settings automatically sync across all devices, so you can continue where you left off.

What's the difference between Creative Mode and Reasoning Mode?

Creative Mode is optimized for artistic and visionary responses, perfect for writing, brainstorming, and creative projects. Reasoning Mode enables deeper problem-solving capabilities, ideal for analysis, research, and logical tasks.

How do I export my conversation history?

Go to Advanced Options > Privacy Controls. Click on "Export Data" and choose your preferred format (PDF, JSON, or TXT). Your complete conversation history will be downloaded to your device.

What languages does Salmar AI support?

Salmar AI supports over 50 languages and dialects. You can switch languages in the settings menu under Language & Tone Settings. The AI will respond in your chosen language with natural fluency.

How do I upgrade to Pro or Enterprise?

Visit the Pricing page and select your desired plan. For Enterprise solutions, contact our sales team for custom pricing and features. All upgrades are instant, and you can downgrade or cancel anytime.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes, you have full control over your data. Go to Privacy Controls and select "Delete Account." This will permanently remove all your data from our servers. This action cannot be undone.
